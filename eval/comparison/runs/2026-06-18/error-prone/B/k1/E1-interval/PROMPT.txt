Implement intersect(A, B) in intersect.js. A and B are each a sorted list of CLOSED integer intervals [start,end] (non-overlapping within a list). Return the list of intervals that are in BOTH A and B (their intersection), sorted. Intervals touching at a single point count: [1,3] and [3,5] intersect at [3,3]. Result intervals must be non-empty (start <= end).
